MEMORY CATCH

    INSTRUCTIONS

  • OBJECTIVE
    The goal of the game is to match all pairs of cards within the given time limit.
  • GAMEPLAY
    Click on any two cards to reveal their symbols. If the symbols match, the cards remain face-up. If not, they flip back over.
  • MATCHING
    Keep track of the symbols on each card you reveal. When you find two matching symbols, they stay face-up, and you earn extra time.
  • EXTRA TIME
    Matching pairs of cards adds 3 seconds to your remaining time.
  • TIMER
    The timer starts counting down from 1 minute (60 seconds). Try to match all pairs before time runs out.
  • WINNING
    You win the game by successfully matching all pairs of cards before the timer reaches zero.
  • HAVE FUN!
    Enjoy the challenge of testing your memory and concentration skills!
